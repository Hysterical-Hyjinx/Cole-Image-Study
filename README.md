Firstly, thank you for wanting to participate in my university dissertation

This is a short university research study involving image selection.
The task runs locally on your computer and is completed quickly.

What you need to do (in order)

1. Unzip the folder:
The study will not function correctly if the folder is not unzipped.

2. Read the ethics information:
Open the Ethics & Consent folder and read through the ethics document.

3. Complete the consent form
Please read, complete, and sign the consent form before continuing.

4. Start the study:
Open the "Image Selection task" (the important part), which will open in your preferred web browser.

5. Complete the task:
Follow the on-screen instructions and complete the task in full.
Ultimately, you will be prompted to download a JSON results file.

6. Email your files:
Please email both of the following: Your completed consent form and your JSON results file

Send them to:
cole.gibson@northumbria.ac.uk

7. Second session 
After approximately one week, you may be contacted via email to repeat the task for a second session. This allows comparison between results over time.

This study:

does not install any software

does not collect personal data

does not connect to the internet

All files run locally on your computer, and you remain in control of what is sent back.

Thank you again for your time and contribution to this research.
